Indian Authors

Indian authors have made significant contributions to the world of literature, producing works that have been acclaimed and celebrated globally. Their writings often explore themes of identity, social justice, and the complexities of human relationships. From ancient epics like the Mahabharata and Ramayana to modern masterpieces by authors like Rabindranath Tagore, R.K. Narayan, and Mahasweta Devi, Indian literature has a rich and diverse tapestry that continues to captivate readers around the world.
